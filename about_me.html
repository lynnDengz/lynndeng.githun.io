My design skills

As a student doing a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Advanced Studies at the University of Sydney, I have created this website as my final project and portfolio for the last semester. Initially, I considered using website templates like WordPress or Wix, but I found it challenging to have fully control over the design. This led me to start a journey to acquire basic knowledge of web design. For this website, I have delved into the world of coding and utilized HTML and CSS as my first steps in practicing coding. This venture has allowed me to gain a foundation in coding principles and techniques. My intention is to further expand my skills by pursuing a Master of Interactive Design, and I am currently dedicating my efforts to mastering the rules of coding in preparation for this next academic pursuit. I possess a diverse skill set that includes traditional painting (watercolor, acrylic), illustration (Procreate, Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint), graphic design (Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign), and video editing (Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere).
