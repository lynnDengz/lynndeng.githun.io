DIGITAL DESIGN

As a graphic designer, I have always been drawn to the mysterious beauty of the moon. The moon’s ever-changing shape has captured the imagination of humans for centuries, inspiring art, literature, and mythology across cultures. In this graphic design booklet, I explore the different phases of the moon and their symbolism through a series of stunning visuals. Each page is a celebration of the moon’s phases, from the waxing crescent to the full moon. Through this project, I aim to convey the powerful emotions and meanings associated with each phase of the moon, such as growth, transformation, reflection, and renewal. By using a combination of bold colors, intricate illustrations. I hope to inspire the viewer to contemplate the beauty and wonder of the moon’s phases, and perhaps even find their own personal connection to this celestial object. Whether you are a lover of astronomy, a spiritual seeker, or simply someone who appreciates beautiful design, I believe this booklet will resonate with you on a deep level.
