Finsiot

During my internship at FINSIOT Technology Co., Ltd. in China, I worked closely with the marketing team as a Graphic Design Intern in the Marketing Department from April 2022 to July 2022. I played an integral role in translating complex concepts into engaging visual content that contributed to the company's marketing strategy.

One of my notable achievements was my involvement in the official website update project. Using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects, I designed the main image and implemented motion effects, resulting in a modern and visually appealing website. This redesign significantly improved user engagement and satisfaction.



Additionally, I created 18 icons using Adobe Illustrator, which enhanced the visual hierarchy of the website and facilitated user navigation. These icons played a crucial role in improving the overall user experience. I also designed eye-catching and informative posters that effectively conveyed messages to the target audience. By utilizing Adobe Illustrator, I developed visually captivating designs that caught the attention of viewers.



Besides, through my internship, I conducted extensive research on similar designs and collaborated closely with the marketing team to develop comprehensive color plans, icon designs, formats, compositions, and motion designs for various projects. This collaborative effort ensured that the visual elements aligned with the company's branding and marketing goals.
