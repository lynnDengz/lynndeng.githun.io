Interdisciplinary Project

To what extent has Instagram impacted Obesity rates

As a team member and designer, I participated in the interdisciplinary project titled "How has Instagram, as a platform for brand marketing and an agent for trendsetting, impacted obesity rates in Australia?" Collaborating with four team members, I took the responsibility of designing the entire slides and visual communication using Canva. I created clear, organized, and easily readable slides that effectively conveyed our research findings and insights. Additionally, I transformed a lengthy 400-word text into two engaging animations using Adobe After Effects, adding visual impact and enhancing the presentation's overall effectiveness. Through these contributions, I aimed to enhance the visual comprehension of our project, ensuring that our research and ideas were effectively communicated to our audience.
